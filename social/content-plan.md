## Content Mix Strategy

### Theme Rotation (Weekly)
- **Week 1:** Textual evidence & hell myth
- **Week 2:** Consciousness & reality
- **Week 3:** Empowerment & transformation
- **Week 4:** Integration & action

### Tone Variation (Daily)
- Mix gentle, bold, empathetic, and prophetic tones
- Balance challenging content with healing messages
- Alternate between revelation and empowerment

### Platform-Specific Optimization

**Instagram:**
- Use carousel posts for multi-point messages
- Stories for quick quotes
- Reels for high-impact hooks
- Focus on visual appeal

**Facebook:**
- Longer captions for deeper engagement
- Discussion posts to encourage comments
- Share personal stories and reflections
- Community building focus

**TikTok:**
- Fast-paced, punchy scripts
- Hook within first 3 seconds
- Use trending sounds when appropriate
- Focus on "aha" moments
