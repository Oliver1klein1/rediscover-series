# Amazon KDP Pricing Recommendations for Rediscover Series

## Market Analysis Summary

Based on research of similar books in the religious/spiritual deconstruction genre:

**Competitor Pricing Ranges:**
- **eBooks:** $3.99 - $9.99 (most common: $6.99 - $9.99)
- **Paperbacks:** $11.95 - $17.95
- **Hardcovers:** Typically $5-8 more than paperback

**Amazon KDP Royalty Structure (2024-2025):**

### eBooks:
- **70% Royalty:** Books priced between **$2.99 - $9.99**
  - Includes delivery cost ($0.15/MB, minimum $0.01)
  - Optimal for maximizing earnings
- **35% Royalty:** Books priced below $2.99 or above $9.99
  - No delivery costs
  - Less profitable unless file size is very large

### Print Books (Paperback & Hardcover):
- **60% Royalty:** Books priced at **$9.99 or higher**
- **50% Royalty:** Books priced below $9.99 (changed June 2025)
- **Printing Costs:** Vary by page count, ink type, and marketplace
  - Example: ~200-page B&W paperback ≈ $4.35 printing cost (US)
  - Longer books (300+ pages) ≈ $5-7 printing cost

---

## Recommended Pricing Strategy

### Overall Strategy:
1. **Price at $9.99+ for print books** to maximize royalty (60% vs 50%)
2. **Price eBooks in the $6.99-$9.99 range** to capture 70% royalty
3. **Consider page count** when setting print prices (longer books can command higher prices)
4. **Maintain price consistency** across the series for brand coherence

## Important Notes

1. **Printing Costs Vary:**
   - Actual printing costs depend on:
     - Page count
     - Color vs. black & white
     - Trim size (6x9" standard)
     - Marketplace (US, UK, EU have different costs)
   - Use KDP's price calculator before finalizing prices

2. **eBook File Size:**
   - Optimize images to minimize delivery costs
   - Large files (>10MB) can significantly reduce 70% royalty earnings
   - Consider using compressed images or removing unnecessary graphics

3. **Market Testing:**
   - Start with recommended prices
   - Monitor sales velocity for 30-60 days
   - Adjust if needed based on:
     - Sales volume
     - Competitor pricing changes
     - Customer feedback

4. **International Pricing:**
   - Amazon automatically converts prices for other marketplaces
   - Consider manual pricing for major markets (UK, CA, AU, EU)
   - Account for VAT/tax differences

5. **Promotional Pricing:**
   - KDP Select allows free promotions and countdown deals
   - Use strategically to boost rankings and reviews
   - Can temporarily drop to $0.99-$2.99 for promotions
