# Rediscover Series Reading Order

Follow this path to experience the movement from fear to freedom.

1. **Liberating Humanity: How Jesus Exposed The Evil God Of Moses And Warned Of Paul** (258 pages)
   - Image: `assets/covers/liberating-humanity-cover.jpg`
   - Dismantles the counterfeit image of God rooted in wrath, blood, and submission.
2. **Escape the Hell Myth: Rediscover The Teachings Of Jesus On Love** (165 pages)
   - Image: `assets/covers/Escape-the-hell-myth-cover-final.jpg`
   - Reveals how mistranslation and control fabricated eternal torment.
3. **101 Illustrated Bible Contradictions: And 12 Keys To Unlock The Truths These Reveal** (341 pages)
   - Image: `assets/covers/bible-contradictions-cover-final.jpg`
   - Uses contradictions as clues, uncovering competing visions of God.
4. **Reality Unveiled: How Jesus Revealed You Are More Powerful Than You Ever Imagined** (260 pages)
   - Image: `assets/covers/reality-unveiled-final.jpg`
   - Explores the radical union of consciousness, science, and Jesus' hidden sayings.
5. **Framing Jesus: How Ancient Bible Changes Elevated Jesus Beyond Our Reach** (162 pages)
   - Image: `assets/covers/framing-jesus-cover-final.jpg`
   - Restores the revolutionary teacher who dismantled oppressive systems.

## Notes
- All titles are available in 6 x 9 inch paperback, hardcover, and ebook formats.
- Swap the order as needed for live events or thematic studies.
- Use this list to build study guides, retreats, and reader journeys.
